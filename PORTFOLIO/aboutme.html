About Me




Get To Know Me!


Graduated with a BS in Biological Science, I spent 4.5 years attending Virginia Tech in the hopes of getting into medical school. However, life doesn't always work out the way you want it to, so I decided to venture into an entirely new career field after I'd graduated.

During my time in college, I dabbled in a bit of coding and took a C++ course. I liked the idea of creating and developing things through programming. So after college, I took a leap of faith and taught myself programming. It was hard, but I was able to understand the basics of programming such as HTML, CSS, etc. In addition, I've taken online courses to get a more structured and extensive understanding of coding. I've used the knowledge that I've gained to create different projects including, a Wordpress blog, a webpage, and much more!
